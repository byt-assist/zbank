🏦 ZBANK — Whitepaper & Liquidity Mechanics

📌 What is ZBANK?

ZBANK is a community-driven token on TON that bridges illiquid and frozen assets into DeFi. We acquire distressed assets — frozen funds, side pockets, NPLs, distressed debt — and convert them into operational liquidity.

🔒 What Assets Do We Put In?

  • 🏦 Frozen funds & side pockets — locked capital from hedge funds, PE, VC
  • 📜 Distressed debt & NPLs — non-performing loans and bankruptcy claims
  • 🥇 Gold (SKR) — warehouse receipts from Global Trust Depository and HSBC
  • 💎 Lab-grown diamonds — IGI/GIA certified, stored in insured vaults
  • 📄 Crypto Bills — promissory notes used as collateral or leased for yield

📈 How Liquidity Grows

We do not simply add liquidity to DEX. We unlock value from illiquid assets. As these assets are monetized, they generate operational liquidity.

📊 Liquidity Growth Formula
If operational liquidity grows by +1%, total ecosystem liquidity grows by +9%

Why +9%?

Because each illiquid asset, when unlocked, creates a multiplier effect through:

  • Rehypothecation — same asset used as collateral multiple times
  • Lending & borrowing — liquidity re-lent to new borrowers
  • Yield generation — fees from escrow, swaps, and leasing
  • Arbitrage — price differences between secondary markets

🔄 How It Works in Practice

  1. Acquire — We buy frozen funds, side pockets, NPLs at 0.5–0.8% of face value
  2. Monetize — Through legal structuring, secondary markets, or restructuring
  3. Generate — Operational liquidity flows into the ZBANK treasury
  4. Amplify — Liquidity is reused, lent, staked, or used for buybacks
