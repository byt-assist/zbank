ZBANK
Whitepaper — Bridging Illiquid Assets into DeFi
📌 1. Introduction

ZBANK is a community-driven token on TON designed to unlock value from illiquid and frozen assets. We acquire distressed assets — frozen funds, side pockets, NPLs, distressed debt — and convert them into operational liquidity.

Unlike traditional tokens, ZBANK is backed by real collateral: gold (SKR), lab-grown diamonds, crypto bills, and institutional-grade loans.

📈 2. Core Mechanism: +1% → +9%

📈 Buy +1% of ZBANK supply

↓ ↓ ↓

(frozen funds, side pockets, NPLs, gold, diamonds, crypto bills)

Why +9%?

Because illiquid assets, when unlocked, create a multiplier effect through:

  • Rehypothecation — same asset used as collateral multiple times
  • Lending & borrowing — liquidity re-lent to new borrowers
  • Yield generation — fees from escrow, swaps, and leasing
  • Arbitrage — price differences between secondary markets
🔒 3. Assets Backing ZBANK

🔹 Frozen Funds & Side Pockets

Locked capital from hedge funds, private equity, and venture capital funds — acquired at 0.5–0.8% of face value.

🔹 Gold (SKR)

Warehouse receipts from Global Trust Depository and HSBC. Fully insured, stored in LBMA-certified vaults.

🔹 Lab-Grown Diamonds

IGI/GIA certified, stored in insured vaults. High liquidity in secondary markets.

🔹 Crypto Bills

Promissory notes used as collateral or leased for yield. Fixed supply, no mint, no freeze.

🔹 Distressed Debt & NPLs

Non-performing loans and bankruptcy claims acquired at deep discount (5–20% of face value).
