🏦 ZBANK — Asset Tokenization Platform

Convert illiquid and frozen assets into liquid ZBANK tokens. Get liquidity in BYT. Pay fees with YBANK or EURF.

  • Frozen funds — locked capital in hedge funds, PE, VC

  • Crypto Bills — promissory notes
