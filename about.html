🏦 About ZBANK

ZBANK is a community-driven token on the TON blockchain designed to unlock value from illiquid and frozen assets. We acquire distressed assets — frozen funds, side pockets, NPLs, distressed debt — and convert them into operational liquidity.

Mission

To bridge illiquid legacy assets into DeFi, providing a transparent, asset-backed token with real-world collateral.

Vision

Create a self-sustaining ecosystem where illiquid assets become productive capital, and every ZBANK holder benefits from the 9x multiplier.

Team

ZBANK is operated by CEBIL UAE, a company registered in the Dubai International Financial Centre (DIFC), with partners including Global Trust Depository, IGI/GIA, STON.fi, and DeDust.

Part of BYT Assist Ecosystem

ZBANK is fully integrated with BYT Assist — a digital asset ecosystem backed by gold, diamonds, institutional escrow, and Islamic finance.
